The Solana Wallet provides a connection to Solana Blockchain.
Out of the box supports OAuth social logins and native biometric logins.

Integrated into most of the top dapps out there from DeFi to NFTs to gaming
apps. Using wallet means giving your users access to the rest of the Solana
ecosystem. Application users can manage their accounts on wallet, head to
[solana.tor.us](https://solana.tor.us) to experience it for yourself. You can also
checkout more [examples of the Wallet integration](integration-guidelines).

user onboarding experience. An overview of features of Wallet:

- Login selection
- Wallet Connect support
- [Private key export](features/exportprivatekey)
- [Themes](features/themes)
- [PWA](features/pwa)
